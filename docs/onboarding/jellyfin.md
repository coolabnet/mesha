# Jellyfin — Community Media Player

## What is Jellyfin?

Jellyfin is the community's media player. It lets you watch films, TV shows, videos, and listen to music that have been added to the community library — directly from your phone, tablet, or computer, without needing an internet connection.

---

## How to Access Jellyfin

Jellyfin works in a browser or through an app. You do not need internet — it runs on the community's own server.

**Address:** Open a browser and go to:

```
http://midia.bairro.local
```

You must be connected to the **community WiFi network**. It will not work on mobile data or other networks.

> **Tip:** On some phones, you may need to type the full address including `http://`. If the page does not open, check that you are connected to the community WiFi and try again.

---

## Logging In

If an account has been created for you, enter your username and password on the login screen and click **Sign In**.

If you do not have an account yet, ask the community maintainer to create one for you. They will give you a username and a starting password.

### Creating your own account (if allowed)

Some Jellyfin setups allow self-registration. If you see a **"Sign up"** or **"New account"** option on the login page:

1. Click **"Sign up"**
2. Choose a username (your name or a nickname)
3. Choose a password
4. Click **"Sign up"** to confirm

If there is no sign-up option, the maintainer creates accounts manually — just ask.

---

## Browsing and Watching Media

After logging in, you will see the media library on the home screen.

### Finding something to watch

1. Browse the **Home** screen to see recently added or recommended content
2. Click on a category (Films, TV Shows, Music, etc.) to browse the full library
3. Use the **Search** icon (magnifying glass) to search by title or name

### Watching a film or video

1. Click on the title you want to watch
2. Click the **Play** button
3. The video will start in your browser

### Controls while watching

- **Pause / Play:** Click the screen or press the spacebar
- **Skip forward/back:** Click on the progress bar at the bottom
- **Volume:** Use the slider in the controls bar
- **Fullscreen:** Click the fullscreen icon in the bottom right corner
- **Subtitles:** Click the speech bubble icon to turn subtitles on or off (if available)

---

## Mobile and TV App Options

The Jellyfin app gives a better experience than the browser, especially on phones and TVs.

### Android phone or tablet

1. Open the **Play Store**
2. Search for **"Jellyfin"**
3. Install the official Jellyfin app
4. Open the app
5. Tap **"Add Server"**
6. Enter the server address: `http://midia.bairro.local`
7. Log in with your username and password

### iPhone (iOS)

1. Open the **App Store**
2. Search for **"Infuse"** or **"Jellyfin"** (Infuse works well with Jellyfin on iOS)
3. Install the app
4. Add the server address: `http://midia.bairro.local`
5. Log in with your username and password

### Android TV / Smart TV

1. Open the TV's app store (Google Play Store or equivalent)
2. Search for **"Jellyfin"**
3. Install the Jellyfin app
4. Enter the server address: `http://midia.bairro.local`
5. Log in

---

## Using Without Internet

Jellyfin works completely without internet. As long as you are connected to the community WiFi, you can watch and listen to everything in the library.

This means you can still watch films and educational videos even when the community's internet connection is down or slow.
